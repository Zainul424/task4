Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action. " - APJ Abdul Kalam

Timeline

  • 1931: Born on October 15th in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India
  • 1954: Graduated in Physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli
  • 1960: Joined the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO)
  • 1980: Directed India's first satellite launch vehicle, SLV-III
  • 1992: Played a pivotal role in India's nuclear tests
  • 2002-2007: Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2015: Passed away on July 27th
